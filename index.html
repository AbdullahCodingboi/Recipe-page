Preparation Time

  • Total time

    Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation

    5 minutes

  • Cooking

    5 minutes

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs

    In a bowl beat the eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed, you can add milk or water

  2. Heat the pan

    Heat a non-stick pan with butter or oil

  3. Add fillings (optional)

    When Eggs begin to set at sides, add your fillings

  4. Fold and serve

    As the Omelette begins to cool, carefully fold it and serve

  5. Enjoy

    Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper
